# Cold Email Templates 📧

## Template 1 — AI Automation (English)
```
Subject: {Company} — saving 10h/week with AI automation

Hi {Name},

I noticed {Company} is growing fast in {sector}.

Most {sector} businesses waste 10+ hours/week on repetitive tasks — lead follow-up, scheduling, reporting.

I built an AI system that automates this completely. It runs 24/7 and costs less than one hour of employee time per month.

Would a 15-min call this week make sense?

Best,
{Your name}
```

## Best Practices
- Subject line: specific + curiosity (never "Partnership opportunity")
- Line 1: prove you researched them
- Body: one problem + one solution
- CTA: low commitment (15 min call, not "buy now")
- Length: max 100 words
- Send: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am local time
